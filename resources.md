## Data and Archives

- [Asian Barometer Survey](https://www.asianbarometer.org/) - cross-national public opinion data for East and Southeast Asia.
- [World Values Survey](https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org/) - comparative public opinion data on political, social, and cultural attitudes.
- [Varieties of Democracy](https://www.v-dem.net/) - democracy, institutions, rights, and political participation indicators.
- [CSES](https://cses.org/) - comparative election study data linking voter surveys with election contexts.

## Methods and Tools

- [R for Data Science](https://r4ds.hadley.nz/) - practical guidance for data import, wrangling, visualization, and modeling in R.
- [Quarto](https://quarto.org/) - reproducible writing and publishing for academic documents, websites, and slides.
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) - static website hosting for project and personal sites.

## Academic Writing

- [The Chicago Manual of Style](https://www.chicagomanualofstyle.org/) - citation and writing style reference.
- [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) - literature search and citation tracking.
